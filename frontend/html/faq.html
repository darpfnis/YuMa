Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Find answers to common questions about the YuMa exchange. If you can't find your answer here, please contact our support team.

General Questions

What is YuMa?

YuMa is a leading cryptocurrency exchange platform that allows users to buy, sell, and trade a variety of digital assets. We strive to provide a secure, user-friendly, and efficient trading experience.

How do I get started with YuMa?

Getting started is easy! Click the "Sign Up" button on our homepage, fill in the required information, and confirm your email address. After creating your account, you can complete identity verification (KYC) to access the full functionality of the platform.

Is the YuMa exchange secure?

Security is our top priority. We employ multi-layered security measures, including cold storage for the majority of assets, two-factor authentication (2FA), advanced encryption, and regular security audits to protect your funds and data.

Account Management

How do I verify my account (KYC)?

To verify your account (Know Your Customer), go to your account settings and find the "Identity Verification" section. You will need to provide a valid government-issued ID and possibly proof of address. Verification helps us comply with regulatory requirements and enhance security.

How do I enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)?

We strongly recommend enabling 2FA. Go to your account's security settings. You can typically use authenticator apps like Google Authenticator or Authy. Scan the QR code or enter the setup key in your app, then enter the code provided by the app to enable 2FA.

What if I forget my password?

If you forget your password, click the "Forgot Password?" link on the login page. Enter your registered email address, and we will send you instructions on how to reset your password.

Deposits and Withdrawals

How do I deposit cryptocurrency?

Go to the "Wallet" or "Assets" page, select the cryptocurrency you wish to deposit, and click "Deposit". You will be provided with a unique deposit address. Send your cryptocurrency to this address from your external wallet or another exchange. Always double-check the address and network.

How long do deposits/withdrawals take?

Deposit times depend on the blockchain network's confirmation speed for the specific cryptocurrency. Withdrawals are processed promptly but also depend on network confirmations. You can usually track the transaction status on a blockchain explorer.

Are there fees or limits for deposits/withdrawals?

We generally do not charge fees for cryptocurrency deposits. Withdrawal fees vary by cryptocurrency and primarily cover network transaction costs. Information on specific fees and limits can be found on our "Fee Schedule" page or in your wallet section.

Trading

How do I buy or sell cryptocurrency on YuMa?

Go to the "Trade" or "Markets" section. Select the trading pair you are interested in (e.g., BTC/USDT). You can then place a "Buy" or "Sell" order using market orders (executed at the current best price) or limit orders (executed at your specified price).

What are the trading fees?

Our trading fees are competitive and are typically based on a maker-taker model. Your fee rate may also depend on your trading volume over the last 30 days. Please refer to our official "Fee Schedule" page for detailed information.

Security

How can I secure my account?

For maximum account protection:

  • Use a strong, unique password.
  • Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA).
  • Beware of phishing attacks – always check the website URL.
  • Do not share your login credentials with anyone.
  • Regularly review your account activity.
What should I do if I suspect unauthorized activity?

If you suspect any unauthorized activity on your account, immediately change your password, review your 2FA settings, and contact our support team for assistance. We will help you secure your account and investigate the issue.

Support

How can I contact customer support?

You can contact our customer support via the "Support" or "Help Center" link on our website. We offer support through online chat, email, or a ticketing system. Please provide as much detail as possible about your issue for a quicker resolution.
